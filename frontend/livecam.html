Use Case
Real-Time Classroom Emotion Monitoring
This live camera mode is designed for educators, trainers, and presenters who want to understand how their audience feels in real time. The system captures frames from your webcam, runs them through a deep learning model (FER-based CNN), and classifies expressions into 7 emotion categories.
Emotion signals are aggregated into an engagement score, tracked over time, and surfaced as trends, dominant emotion chips, and AI-generated insights — helping you adapt your delivery on the fly.
