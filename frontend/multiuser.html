Use Case
Multi-User Group Emotion Monitoring
Designed for educators, trainers, and facilitators who need to track emotional states across an entire room simultaneously. A single camera captures the full group — the model detects every human face independently and classifies each into 7 emotion categories in real time.
Only forward-facing participants are captured — background elements and non-human subjects are strictly excluded. Square bounding boxes with emotion labels are drawn live on each detected face.
